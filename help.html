Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is this website about?
A: This website is a platform where users can upload their own experiences about culture, food, safety precautions, a nd entertainment in each borough of New York City. You can read about other people's experiences and share your own to help others explore and discover the city.
Q: How do I navigate the website?
A: The website is organized by boroughs. You can select a borough from the homepage and browse through the different submissions which are categorized by culture, food, safety, and entertainment.
Q: Can I submit my own experiences to the website?
A: Yes, you can submit your own experiences by heading to the Submit Experience tab, you can write about your experiences for any of the boroughs in the city.
Q: How can I ensure that my submissions are accurate and helpful to other users?
A: Please make sure that your submissions are based on your own experiences and provide as much detail as possible. It's also helpful to include the location, date, and any other relevant information about your experience. Please be respectful and avoid posting anything offensive or inappropriate.
Q: Can I contact other users through the website?
A: No, the website does not have a messaging feature. However, you can up-vote each other's submissions as a form of feedback.
Q: Are the submissions on the website moderated?
A: Yes, all submissions are moderated to ensure that they are appropriate and helpful for other users. If a submission violates the website's guidelines, it will be removed.
Q: How can I report inappropriate content or behavior on the website?
A: You can report inappropriate content or behavior by contacting our moderators through the email on our About page. The website moderators will review the report and take appropriate action if necessary.
Q: How can I stay up-to-date with new submissions and features on the website?
A: You can follow the website's social media accounts or sign up for the newsletter to receive updates on new submissions and features. You can also check the Recent Submissions section on the homepage for featured submissions or browse through the latest submissions on each borough page by scrolling down at the end of the page.

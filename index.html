Queens, Entertainment

Went to Newtown HQ in Astoria, this is such a cute spot! It's got a little something cute for everyone. I love their assortment of stickers, especially the corgi stuff they just got. I also picked up a folding backpack type bag that I've had my eye on for a while. Although it's available elsewhere I really wanted to support a local shop. Check them out!

Manhattan, Food

Went to Red Rooster in Harlem, Food 100 service 100 ambiance 1000!!!! Visiting from Texas and I am currently here with a friend. The catfish was to die for!!!!! Waiting on theSe crab cakes will update!!!!! 10/10 highly recommend music is great and the people are even better.

Brooklyn, Culture

Went to the International African Arts festival in Fulton. I was absolutely thrilled to be back at festival this year… Everyone that I interacted with was beyond elated to be in each other's presence. It's literally like a homecoming in that many people actually travel back to Brooklyn to participate! And… the suggested price of $5 for adults and $2 for children is more than reasonable… (No one stops you if you don't have it. ) Looking forward to next year!!
